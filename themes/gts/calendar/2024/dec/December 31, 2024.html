Grim's latest endeavors have struck a chord among fans, with voices both supporting and criticizing his actions and role in the show.

With a mix of fervent support and fervent disdain, many vocal fans expressed their dissatisfaction over Grim's potential insistence on re-establishing his champion status and his continued prominence in GTS’s main events. This discontent is compounded by a general sentiment that Grim should step aside to allow fresh talent to shine. However, Grim is resistant to these critiques and makes it clear that GTS and Grim are synonymous, expressing that without him, GTS wouldn't retain its essence.

Conflict with Duhop: Brotherly Strife
An enduring narrative in this episode is the sibling rivalry between Grim and his brother. Grim vehemently accuses his brother of an unscrupulous attack that left him with a damaged knee. The accusation is straightforward: Grim believes Duhop ambushed him, sporting a Man in Black disguise. This added injury complicates Grim's return to the wrestling ring, hindering his preparations for upcoming significant matches, notably the TF Crew Rumble in Connecticut and the Regal Rumble.

Despite being injured, Grim maintains a determined spirit, vowing to continue his wrestling journey. He plans to partake in events, though he remains cautious about cashing in opportunities prematurely and risking further setbacks. Grim is resolute to win the Regal Rumble and claim an accolade that has eluded him so far. Grim passionately reiterates his role as the cornerstone of GTS. According to him, without his persona, GTS loses its edge and uniqueness.

Grim throws some punches, metaphorically, at other wrestlers and promotions, denouncing their capabilities. He sarcastically suggests watching other wrestlers fumble through their shows if fans don’t appreciate his work, thus making it clear that GTS’s essence is intertwined with his success.

TF Crew Rumble and The Regal Rumble
Grim’s dedication towards reclaiming his dominant status is evident as he discusses participating in the TF Crew Rumble and preparing for the Regal Rumble.
